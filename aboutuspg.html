About Us

There are so many people who message us everyday and inquire what our shop is all about, and therefore we believe an introduction is needed. We’re a customer-friendly, service oriented bike shop expert in dealing with all kinds of your biking needs. We simply do our very best to ensure that our shop is a comfortable zone for people especially those who are new to cycling to learn more, and additionally for experienced cyclists to update their skills and get the most up-to-date bike parts.

Vision

Crown Bicycles envisions a world in which millions of people ride bicycles. In villages all around Sri Lanka, bicycling is a commonplace pastime. In Sri Lanka, bicycling is accepted, accommodated, and subsidized as a legal and necessary means of transportation. Residents of Crown enjoy a healthy and active lifestyle, which includes safe and convenient active transportation alternatives. Crowns Bicycle communities gain from greater health, stronger economies, and a higher quality of life as a consequence of this initiative. Crown has set the standard for innovative bicycle-friendly transportation systems and initiatives across the country.

Mission

Crown Bicycles is dedicated to increasing bicycle accessibility, safety, and education. We support and promote bicycling, as well as enhanced safety and circumstances. We bring people together, motivate them, and give cyclists in Sri Lanka a united voice.
